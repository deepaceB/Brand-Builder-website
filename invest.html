FAQ

Questions investors ask.

What types of investments do you accept?
We consider equity investment, strategic partnerships, and product-specific funding. Structure depends on the venture and the stage it's at — we'll walk through the options in an initial discussion.
Can I invest in a single product?
Yes. Several products in the portfolio can take direct investment on their own, separately from the wider group. Others are tied more closely to shared platform infrastructure — we'll tell you which is which up front.
Do you offer equity partnerships?
We do. Equity terms vary by product and stage, and are agreed during the partnership stage of the process after due diligence is complete.
Are client companies independently managed?
Yes. The partner companies listed on this page are independently owned and managed. We work alongside them as a technology and growth partner, and introduce opportunities where there's a genuine fit.
How do I request more information?
Message us on WhatsApp or email mail@brandbuilder.com.np with the products or companies you're interested in. We'll share the relevant material and set up a call.
